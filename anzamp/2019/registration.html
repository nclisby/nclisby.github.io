Registration

Please use the form below to register for the meeting. This form is embedded from trybooking.com and is a secure connection.

Earlybird registration fees and conference dinner prices for the meeting are:

Standard rate ANZAMP member rate Retired ANZAMP member rate ANZAMP student member rate
Registration $250 $200 $100 $0
Conference dinner $80 $65 $65 $45

All prices are in Australian Dollars (AUD).

Early bird deadline: Saturday 1st December 2018
After the early bird deadline the standard and ANZAMP member registration fees increase by $100.

Abstract submission deadline: Saturday 1st December 2018
Participants wishing to present their work must submit an abstract by this date. Abstracts can be submitted as part of the registration process or at a later time via the abstract submission form on this website. Late abstracts and updates or corrections to submitted abstracts can still be accepted subject to scheduling and program printing constraints. Only one abstract will be accepted per participant. To replace a previous abstract, simply use the abstract submission form again.

Please report problems using the registration form to the organisers at anzamp.meeting@gmail.com.
