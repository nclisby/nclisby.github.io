Abstract submission

To register for the Sixth Annual Meeting of the Australian and New Zealand Associating of Mathematical Physics (ANZAMP 2018) please use the registration form.

Use the form on this page if you have already registered for the meeting and still need to submit an abstract.

If you wish to update your abstract, simply fill out this form again with the same name and email address. Your most recent abstract will be the one that is used in the program.

The abstract submission deadline is Sunday 17th December 2017.
