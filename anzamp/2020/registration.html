CODE OF CONDUCT
ANZAMP is a division within the Australian Mathematical Society (AustMS). It is a condition of registration that all delegates read, and agree to act in accordance with, the AustMS Code of Conduct. A copy is available at

www.austms.org.au/Code+Of+Conduct

Please use the form below to register for the meeting. This form is embedded from trybooking.com and is a secure connection.

Earlybird registration fees and conference dinner prices for the meeting are:

Standard rate ANZAMP member rate Retired ANZAMP member rate ANZAMP student member rate
Registration $370 $270 $190 $0
Conference dinner $90 $75 $75 $50

All prices are in Australian Dollars (AUD). Registration includes admission to the welcome reception, as well as morning tea, lunch, and afternoon tea throughout the meeting.

Early bird deadline: Saturday 30th November 2019
After the early bird deadline the standard and ANZAMP member registration fees increase by $100.

Abstract submission deadline: Sunday 1st December 2019
Participants wishing to present their work must submit an abstract by this date. Abstracts can be submitted as part of the registration process or at a later time via the abstract submission form on this website. Late abstracts and updates or corrections to submitted abstracts can still be accepted subject to scheduling constraints. Only one abstract will be accepted per participant. To replace a previous abstract, simply use the abstract submission form again.

Please report problems using the registration form to the organisers at anzamp.meeting@gmail.com.
