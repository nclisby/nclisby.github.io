Transport and accommodation

Venue

We acknowledge the traditional owners of the lands on which we will meet, the Bundjalung and Yugambeh people.

The meeting will be held at Twin Towns Conference and Function Centre (Tweed Heads, New South Wales) from Wednesday February 5 to Friday February 7, 2020. There will be a welcome reception on the evening of Tuesday February 4, and conference dinner on the evening of Thursday February 6. Both events will be at Twin Towns.

To assist in navigating the venue, delegates can consult this map .

Transport

Nearby Gold Coast Airport (OOL) is an international airport. Note that the airport is in Queensland, operating under a different time zone (Australian Eastern Time) to that of the conference venue in New South Wales (Australian Eastern Daylight Saving Time).

The airport is situated less than five kilometres from the conference venue. A taxi or ride-sharing service is an inexpensive option for travel after arrival. Further information about commuting between the airport and Tweed Heads is available through the Gold Coast Airport webpage.

Accommodation

We have negotiated discounted accommodation rates, with options for buffet breakfast included, through Mantra Resort Twin Towns. The resort is situated across the road from the conference venue, with pedestrian access via an elevated walkway.

To take advantage of this accommodation offer, delegates must download and complete this booking form and follow the submission instructions to make their reservation.
