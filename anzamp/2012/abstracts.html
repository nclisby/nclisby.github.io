Abstracts

We will add titles and abstracts to this page as they are received. Please send your title and abstract to anzamp.meeting@gmail.com with `abstract' as the subject, and we'll add it to the list.

Placeholders have been created for talks for which we have titles from the registration form. Additional information, such as the names of collaborators, can be included if you wish.

Note that whiteboard facilities are limited. Please prepare your talk electronically as a PDF file for video projection, and bring it to the venue on a memory stick.
