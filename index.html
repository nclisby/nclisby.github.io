Nathan Clisby's homepage

Welcome to my homepage! I'm a mathematical physicist in the School of Mathematics and Statistics at the University of Melbourne, supported by an ARC Future Fellowship. I'm also an associate investigator of MASCOS.

Here you will find some details about my research (including lists of possible research topics for students and collaborators, and my publications), and various project pages where you can find interactive applications, visualisations, and in future software for download.

This is a long overdue overhaul of my old homepage, which had fallen into disrepair. With the goal of maintaining the new version indefinitely (at least the url's), I've chosen to create a simple website using only html, css, and javascript. My apologies that there are still a few rough edges! In particular I'd like to find time to both make my research more accessible to a broader audience, and to add more depth for those who are interested.
