Details

International visitors

We highly recommend you visit the "Visa" page on the website of Statphys 24 (http://www.statphys.org.au/) for information on visa requirements for travel to Australia. On that page they indicate that if you do require a visa, it is advisable to apply at least 6-8 weeks prior to traveling to Australia.

Location

The conference will be held in the Executive Lounge on Level 1 of the Alan Gilbert Building, The University of Melbourne, 161 Barry St, Carlton, on the corner of Grattan St and Barry St. Link to Google map: http://bit.ly/bfJcOU.

Fees

The conference fee is AUD$100, to be paid either in advance by credit card or cheque, or by cash or cheque at registration on July 26.

Conference dinner

The conference dinner will be at Bokchoy Tang on July 27 at 7pm, a Chinese restaurant on the banks of the Yarra river in the Melbourne CBD. The dinner will cost AUD$60, including drinks, with a vegetarian option. See http://www.bokchoytang.com.au/.

Getting here

Travel by plane from Statphys 24 from Cairns to Melbourne, either directly (relatively few flights available) or via Brisbane or Sydney. Flights are available from Virgin Blue, Qantas and Jetstar. An aggregator site which simultaneously searches all domestic flights is Webjet.
