Treacherous Tower

You have found both the diamond necklace and the other jewellery! Jubilantly, you head back towards the main entrance, hoping to meet the mysterious owner.

As you turn the corner you see that there is a huge party going on outside. Everyone says “Here they are!” which doesn't make sense to you because you are alone. But then you notice Wispy the reptile-dog has followed you. Your friends tip Wispy on his back and unzip a strange zipper in his fur, revealing your dog, Blossom, inside! She gives you a giant lick!

You ask “What is this all about? I know it's my birthday but what about the tower? Who is the mysterious owner?” Your mother hands you a letter which explains everything:

Dear friend,

The owners of Treacherous Tower are your mother and father. This tower has been hidden in the forest for hundreds of years. We moved the path, and built Flora the robot, who we designed to come to life when any crumbly powder is placed on her. Like your fairy dust, which was actually just plain sand.

This whole thing was set up to give you a memorable birthday, and we hope that you found all of the sweets and presents inside! We also made this birthday surprise because we have one big secret which we are about to reveal: this tower will be yours when you turn 18! And until then you can play in it as much as you like, but you can't redecorate it for the moment.

From your Mum and Dad, the owners of Treacherous Tower.

You say with a smile “I wouldn't redecorate Treacherous Tower anyway.”


The End
