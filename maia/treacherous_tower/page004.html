Treacherous Tower

The reason you had the fairy dust and knew how to use it was that it fell out of the sky into your bag, and you felt the difference in weight in your bag and read the label. You had thought this was a strange prank from your friends, but with the appearance of the mysterious tower you are now wondering if the fairy dust might actually be real.

You take a pinch of fairy dust and sprinkle it over the swan statue. The swan spreads its majestic wings, its body beating with life. And it begins to speak!

Hi. I'm Flora. I thought I was going to be stuck as a statue forever, thank you so much for rescuing me. Since you seem to be not too heavy, I will give you one ride to any place you wish. Where would you like to go?
You can't believe your luck! This swan is willing to take you wherever you want to go. Of course you choose to go to the window that would lead you into the tower.

As you clamber through the window you thank Flora and say goodbye.
