Treacherous Tower

After proceeding down the left icy path, you enter a resplendent throne room. You are strongly confident that you made the right choice, because you can see the burglars' footprints. But did they just come here to lay a trap?

You step on some unusually high ground. There is a weight coming down towards the ground nearby, and you realise that you are standing on a lever. You are so shocked that you cannot react in time. Lucky for you the board falls of its fulcrum and you are saved. You run back down the icy pathway and this time take the right-hand path.
