Treacherous Tower

You examine the tower carefully, trying to find an entrance. You try the door handle and are surprised to find the door unlocked. It also makes you a bit suspicious. Why would the door be unlocked - is it a trap? There is also a swan statue, which if it were alive would be large enough to carry you through a window that is open in the tower. That would also be possible, even if it were only a statue, because there is a bag of fairy dust which is supposed to bring statues to life in your bag (even though you have not tried it yet).


Do you choose to bring the statue to life and ride the statue through the window, or open the front door and walk into the tower that way.
