Treacherous Tower

You carefully pick up the piece of paper, certain that you have made the right choice. But, a bony hand reaches up from under the table! You slap it away; it was only a robot hand which could not possibly harm you, it was only designed to scare you away. Luckily a skeletous hand would not scare you like that, because you have always liked Halloween.

The answer to the riddle was the envelope! Because it starts with an ‘e’ and ends with an ‘e’, and contains a letter - just not a letter from the alphabet!
