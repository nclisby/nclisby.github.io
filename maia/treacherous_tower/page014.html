Treacherous Tower

You know this must be the second to last part of this passage of the journey.

You are suspecting this is the dining room. But then you notice a low-pitched growl.

You must face the final male of the giant reptile-dog, which has scales on its tail, but the rest of its body is furry and warm. The reptile-dog belongs to Nancy, so it is against you. But you smile; you realise that you do not have to make the last, final choice that the owner of treacherous tower recommended. You know that the first thing that every giant male reptile-dog is looking for is a mate, and the second thing is food. They are actually not carnivores, and their favourite food is cooked carrot. Luckily, you took a carrot with you for a snack on the bushwalk, and it cooked when you went down the boiling passage!
