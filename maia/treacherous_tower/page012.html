Treacherous Tower

You pick up the envelope and nothing suspicious happens. You open the envelope and immediately start reading out the letter to yourself:

Dear friend,

Nancy was not much of a skilled burglar, although she did find a good hiding place for my jewellery!

You must run up the path behind the table.

From your friend, the owner of Treacherous Tower.
P.s.: The only reason why Nancy's riddle trap had never failed was because she had never actually tried it before!
